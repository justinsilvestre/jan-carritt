Guitarist and Vocalist

Jan Carrit is an Arizona-based musician who specializes in covering country and folk classics.

Since studying under famed songwriter Tim Hardin in 1963, Jan has made appearances at venues all across the country, both as a soloist and with his band, the Redneck Roundup Band.

Jan has lived in the Tuscon area since 1981, but he continues to travel and would be happy to play at a venue near you
