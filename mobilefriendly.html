Guitarist and Vocalist

Jan Carrit is an Arizona-based musician who specializes in heartfelt renditions of country and folk classics.

Since studying under famed songwriter Tim Hardin in 1963, Jan has made appearances at venues all across the country, both as a soloist and with his band, the Redneck Roundup Band.

Jan has lived in the Phoenix area since 1981, but he continues to travel and enjoys playing music wherever the road takes him.
